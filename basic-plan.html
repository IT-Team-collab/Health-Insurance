Basic Plan Details

$199/month

Primary Care Coverage

Covers regular check-ups and visits to your primary care physician with a $25 copay per visit. Includes:

  • Annual physical examinations
  • Basic health screenings
  • Vaccinations and immunizations
  • Treatment for common illnesses

Emergency Services

Comprehensive emergency care coverage with a $150 copay per emergency room visit. Includes:

  • 24/7 emergency room access
  • Ambulance services
  • Urgent care visits ($50 copay)
  • Emergency surgical procedures

Prescription Drug Coverage

Three-tier prescription drug coverage system:

  • Generic medications: $10 copay
  • Preferred brand medications: $30 copay
  • Non-preferred brand medications: $50 copay
  • 90-day supply available for maintenance medications

Preventive Care

100% coverage for preventive services with no copay. Includes:

  • Annual wellness visits
  • Routine blood work
  • Cancer screenings
  • Blood pressure monitoring
